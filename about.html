Our Story

Azad Enterprises was founded with a simple goal — make quality hardware and plumbing materials easily available to the people of Juhu and the surrounding suburbs of Mumbai. What started as a small neighbourhood shop has grown into one of the most trusted material suppliers in the area.

With over 10+ years in business, we serve plumbers, contractors, interior designers, builders, cooperative housing societies and homeowners. Our deep product range, fair pricing and same-day delivery have made us a go-to partner for projects big and small.
